Guardians are the steadfast protectors of Middle-earth, specializing in tanking.

Roles: Tank (Blue), ST DPS (Red), AoE DPS (Yellow)

Guardians excel at absorbing damage and protecting their allies. They use heavy armor and shields to withstand powerful attacks.

  • Blue Line: Focuses on tanking and threat generation
  • Red Line: Emphasizes single-target damage

Getting Started

Character Creation

Choose a race and origin that affects your character's starting zone and appearance. Each race has several origins, which change the character's appearance slightly.

Choose an appearance by selecting hair style, skin tone, and other features. You can change some appearance items later at a Barber.

Select a name for your character, ensuring it follows the lore and guidelines for the chosen race. The game allows you to change your name later for a fee.
